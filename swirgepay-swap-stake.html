SwirgePay Staking

The SwirgeBar located app.swirge.com/staking allows you to stake your Swirge and receive xSwirge in return and then you stake it in the xSwirge pool.

When users make trades on the SwirgeSwap Exchange a 0.3% fee is charged.

0.05% of this fee is added to the SwirgeiBar pool in the form of LP tokens for the relative pool.

When the rewards contract is called (minimum once per day) all the LP tokens are sold for Swirge (on SwirgeSwap Exchange). The newly purchased Swirge is then divided up proportionally between the xSwirge holders in the pool, meaning their xSwirge is now worth more Swirge.

Currently you will not see the gained amount until you have withdrawn.

It started as 1 Swirge = 1 xSwirge, but just like LP tokens the price of xSwirge changes over time depend on how many Swirge rewards are in the pool.

Swirge Social

Swirge social is a decentralized social media platform where one can get to meet new people, chat, share their thoughts and ideas, and also stay updated on the happenings around you. On Swirge social, its users have full control of everything as no one has the right to bring down your posts. The community has full control of everything. You get to be in control of your chats as no one can see them. You can create ur own blogs and gain followers on Swirge social. Express yourself and share your thoughts freely on Swirge Social.

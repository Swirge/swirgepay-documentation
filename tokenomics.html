Tokenomics

Why does $Swirge have infinite inflation?

$Swirge no longer has infinite inflation - the community voted for a hard cap of 250M tokens.


What is the cap of the $Swirge token?

A hard cap of 250M has been approved - but there are not yet contractual changes in place to create this hard cap.

Is there a lockup on earned $Swirge?

Right now, the plan is to have 2/3rds of $Swirge earned go into a lockup address and be later distributed to the LPs who earned it from the farm.

Does the $Swirge token have any burn?

No not at at this time - but once can be proposed through Governance channels.

How does $Swirge enter into circulation?

New Swirge is created at 100 Swirge per block for tokens that are staked in the farms on Swirgeswapclassic.org/farms

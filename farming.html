Swirge Pay Farms

The Swirge Pay Farms are located here:

  • https://app.swirgeswap.com

The Farm allows users to yield farm Swirge rewards with each new block based on staking SLP tokens they received from app.swirgepay.com/pool
The farm previously supported UNI-V2 LP tokens from Uniswap but it now only works with tokens from SushiSwap pools.

Users enter into the farms of their choice.
When farming users will receive a new distribution of Sushi with every block. In the first two weeks of launch the rewards were 1000 Sushi per block, divided by the proportional share of the pool.
The exception to this was the "Sushi Party!" pool which had an additional 2x bonus.
After the first two weeks the reward is set to drop to 100 Sushi per block.
The APY calculation is based on the return in ETH to the daily USD value, based on current block return annualized. It is not a fixed return, it is not a promised return, it is not financial advice. It is based on ETH returns.

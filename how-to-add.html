How to add tokens to SwirgeSwap Exchange as an LP

Step 1 Go to https://app.swirgepay.com Click the large "Add Liquidity" button

Step 2 Select which token pair you want to add liquidity for

Step 3 Fill in the amounts you want to add to the liquidity pool. It may ask you to "Approve" the tokens - you will have to send these "approve transactions" first Once you've approved the token you can hit "Supply" Then a transaction will pop-up for you to sign in your wallet (like MetaMask) - after the transaction is complete you will have successfully added liquidity.

Step 4 The transaction will provide you with some "SLP" tokens in your wallet. These are Swirgeswap LP tokens and represent the share you've deposited in the pool. All pools are labeled as SLP but represent different pools. When you go back to the app.swirgepay.com/#/pool page you will now see your

Step 5 If you click the arrow, the drop-down menu will show you more details about the pool including your shares, and options to add or remove liquidity.

Congratulations, you have successfully staked your tokens as a "Liquidity Provider" in SwirgeSwap. This means when people make a trade you will be eligible for part of the 0.25% trade reward.

How does the trade fee reward work?

Every time a user trades between $Swirge and $ETH a 0.3% fee is taken on the trade. 0.25% of that trade goes back to the the LP pool.

If previously there were 100 SLP tokens representing 100 ETH and 100 Swirge each token would be worth 1 ETH & 1 SRM.

If one user trade 10 ETH for 10 Swirge, and another traded 10 Swirge for 10 ETH, then there would now be 100.025 ETH and 100.025 Swirge.

This means each LP token would be worth 1.00025 ETH and 1.00025 Swirge now when it is withdrawn.

Does this mean I am also farming new Swirge?

No. Once you have the SLP tokens you'll need to go to Swirgeswapclassic.org/farms to also farm them for new Swirge rewards each block.

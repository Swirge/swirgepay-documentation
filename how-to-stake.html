How to Stake Swirge for xSwirge

Step 1:

Go to app.swirge.com/staking - and unlock your wallet.

Step 2:

From there you'll be able to see the "Swirge Bar" where you can stake Swirge (turning it into xSwirge) in order to earn part of the 0.05% trade fee from all pools on the exchange.

Step 3:

First, you may need to "Approve" the Swirge token before you are able to use it. Simply hit the "Approve SWG" button and sign the transaction with your wallet or MetaMask.

Step 4:

Once the Swirge token is approved you'll be able to hit "Convert to xSwirge" to stake your Swirge.

Step 5:

Then a new window prompt will pop-up letting you select how much SWIRGE you want to stake. Select the amount, hit confirm and send the transaction.

Step 6:

After that you will see the xSwirge available in the bar.

The amount of xSWIRGE in the SwirgeBar will not change. But the amount of Swirge it represents will.

As fees are collected they are converted to $SWIRGE and distributed proportionally across holders in the xSWIRGE pool. So when you withdraw your xSWIRGE back into $SWIRGE it will be worth more $SWIRGE than when you put in.

These rewards are slower to update (because they cost a lot of gas) and so you should expect them to update, on average, once per day.

Currently there is no way in the UI to see the level of rewards until you withdraw. The team is working to fix this.
